◆ TIMER1_WGM_t

enum TIMER1_WGM_t

Enum that provide the options of waveform generation mode of timer 1.

  • NORMAL_16_BITS For normal WGM with a top at 0xFFFF.
  • PHASE_CORR_8_BITS For phase correct PWM WGM with a top at 0x00FF.
  • PHASE_CORR_9_BITS For phase correct PWM WGM with a top at 0x01FF.
  • PHASE_CORR_10_BITS For phase correct PWM WGM with a top at 0x03FF.
  • CTC_OCR1A For CTC WGM with a top at OCR1A register value.
  • FAST_PWM_8_BITS For fast PWM WGM with a top at 0x00FF.
  • FAST_PWM_9_BITS For fast PWM WGM with a top at 0x01FF.
  • FAST_PWM_10_BITS For fast PWM WGM with a top at 0x03FF.
  • PHASE_FREQ_CORR_ICR1 For phase and frequency correct WGM with a top at ICR1 register value.
  • PHASE_FREQ_CORR_OCR1A For phase and frequency correct WGM with a top at OCR1A register value.
  • PHASE_CORR_ICR1 For phase correct PWM WGM with a top at ICR1 register value.
  • PHASE_CORR_OCR1A For phase correct PWM WGM with a top at OCR1A register value.
  • CTC_ICR1 For CTC WGM with a top at ICR1 register value.
  • FAST_PWM_ICR1 For PWM WGM with a top at ICR1 register value.
  • FAST_PWM_OCR1A For PWM WGM with a top at OCR1A register value.

enum TIMERS_OC_MODE_t

Enum that provides the options of compare match output.

  • OC_DISCONNECTED To disconnect any of OC1x pins.
  • TOG_OC To toggle OC1x pins.
  • CLR_OC To clear OC1x pins.
  • SET_OC To set OC1x pins.
  • NON_INVERTED_PWM For non inverted PWM.
  • INVERTED_PWM For inverted PWM.
  • DO_NOT_CHANGE To not change the current mode.
